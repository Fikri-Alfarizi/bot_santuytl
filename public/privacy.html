Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines what data SantuyTL collects and how it is used.

What Data We Collect

  • Discord User ID: To identify you within the system.
  • Server ID: To manage guild-specific settings.
  • Activity Data: Such as XP, Levels, Coins, and Items inventory.

How We Use Data

This data is used solely for bot functionality (e.g., tracking your rank, economy balance, and preferences).

We do not share your data with third parties or use it for advertising.

No real personal data (names, addresses, photos) is collected beyond what is publicly available via the Discord API.

Data Removal

Users may request full data deletion by contacting the development team or owner via the Discord Server.
